Pillar 2 — income allocation as optimization Pillar 2 — 收入分配本身就是个优化问题

Multinational tax planning often treats each jurisdiction's income as a fixed input. In practice, transfer pricing rules frequently define an acceptable range rather than a single point. Allocation within those ranges can affect the final tax outcome, so I modeled it as an optimization problem.
做跨国税务的人一般把每个法域的收入当成定死的，但其实不是——转让定价给你的是一个区间，不是一个点。你在这些区间里怎么摆，直接影响最后交多少税。既然是区间，那就是个优化问题。我写了个框架来解它。

German CbCR automation 德国 CbCR 自动申报

End-to-end pipeline for Country-by-Country Reporting to the German BZSt. Reconciles multi-entity, multi-currency financial data, generates XML Schema 2.0 compliant files, handles the ELMA5 → DIP portal migration, DocRefId formatting, Steuernummer validation. In production for live client filings. 给德国联邦税务局（BZSt）做的国别报告全流程自动化。多实体、多币种的财务数据勾稽，生成 XML Schema 2.0 的申报文件，ELMA5 到 DIP 的系统切换、DocRefId、税号校验这些坑全踩过。已经在客户实际申报里跑了。
